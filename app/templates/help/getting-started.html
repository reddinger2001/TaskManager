GETTING STARTED

Step 1: Capture a Task

The fastest way to add a task is the quick-capture modal. Press Ctrl+K or / from anywhere in the app, type a title, and hit Enter.

Tasks land in your Inbox — unorganized, no project, no priority. That's fine. The point is speed: get it out of your head first, organize later.

Step 2: Triage Your Inbox

When you have a moment, click on inbox tasks to open the detail view. Here you can:

  • Assign it to a Project
  • Set a Priority (P0–P2 or none)
  • Change the Status (Backlog → Active → Done)
  • Add a Due date
  • Add Tags, links, or a description

Step 3: Work Your System

The workflow is simple:

  1. Capture — anything that comes to mind goes in the Inbox
  2. Triage — assign project, priority, and status
  3. Process — move Backlog → Active when you're ready to work on it
  4. Complete — mark as Done when finished

The Dashboard Is Your Pulse Check

Open the dashboard and in 5 seconds you should know:

  • Are there any fires (P0 tasks)?
  • What am I actively working on?
  • Is anything overdue?
  • How are my projects doing?

TIP: The big number cards at the top of the dashboard are clickable. Click "Active" to see all active tasks, click "On Fire" to filter by P0, etc.
