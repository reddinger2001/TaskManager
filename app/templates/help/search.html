SEARCH

TaskManager uses SQLite's full-text search (FTS5) to search across tasks, projects, and logs.

Keyword Search

Searches task titles, descriptions, and tags. Also searches project names and log entries by title and notes.

Use the search bar in the header to find anything by keyword. Results are grouped by type: Tasks, Projects, and Logs.

Related Captures

When viewing a task detail, the "Related Captures" section uses keyword search to find inbox tasks that share similar content — useful for connecting related thoughts.
